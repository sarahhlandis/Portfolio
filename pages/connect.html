Hey there!
If you've found this page, hopefully you're wanting to know more about me as a person. If you're in the wrong spot and are looking for more information about me in the workplace, check out my 'me' (above) or 'about' (below) section.

So... a bit about me. I was actually born in India, and was adopted by an American family - my parents were both computer engineers but until recently, I actually had no idea what that entailed. I just knew they were very smart. My dad had an IBM Thinkpad in the 90s which was pretty high tech for the early days and I just remember vigorously pressing the little red mouse in the middle of the keyboard to see how fast it could travel across the screen.

Fast-forward to now and I always seem to be the one in the group who's able to magically press a few buttons on most electronic devices and voilà - the item is working again! I've always liked computers and their advanced capabilities so it seems fitting that I've taken the route in synthesizing design and technology, in whatever way that may be.

Since moving to Australia from Washington D.C. (USA), I've been lucky enough to gain a greater appreciation for the general outdoors. I enjoy spending time in the sun and near the ocean. I try to live a mindful and purposeful daily life, with balance and energy. I spend a lot of time doing pilates and riding horses (when I can)! My husband and I also love to travel and experiencing new places. A fun fact about me - I've gone cage-floor diving with great white sharks off the coast of South Australia!

If you'd like to know more or think we'd be a good team for a project in the professional domain, feel free to click the contact button and send me a message!
